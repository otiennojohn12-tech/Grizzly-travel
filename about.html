Who We Are

Kenya isn't just our destination — it's our home.

Grizzly Travel was born from a simple belief: the real Kenya is not found in a brochure. It's in the red dust of Tsavo, in the rhythm of a Luo fishing village at dawn, in the silence of the Mara at first light.

We are a Nairobi-based travel brand specializing in cultural and heritage tourism across Kenya. Our founders grew up in these landscapes, know these communities personally, and believe that travel should connect people — not just to places, but to stories.

Every itinerary we design is built from the ground up — no cookie-cutter packages, no inflated middlemen. Just honest, expert-guided Kenya experiences at prices that make sense.
