FAQ

Common Questions

How do I confirm a booking?

Message us on WhatsApp with your preferred package, dates, and group size. We'll send a booking confirmation and payment details.

Are the prices per person or per group?

Prices listed are per person, based on shared accommodation. We offer group discounts for 4+ travellers — ask us for a quote.

Can I customize a package?

Absolutely. We specialize in bespoke itineraries. Tell us your interests, budget, and dates and we'll build something unique.

What's included in the packages?

Accommodation, game drives or tours, most meals, park fees, and a local guide. International flights are not included unless specified.

What's the cancellation policy?

We offer free cancellation up to 14 days before departure. Within 14 days, a partial fee applies. Contact us for details.
